Dhaka

Dhaka is the capital and largest city of Bangladesh. With its colorful history and rich cultural traditions.

Comilla

Comilla has a total area of 3085.17 square kilometres. It is bounded by Burichang and Tripura on the north.

Chittagong

It is officially called Chattogram.It lies about 12 miles north of the mouth of the Karnaphuli River.

Rajshahi

Rajshahi is an important administrative, cultural and business centre in Bangladesh today.

Khulna

Khulna is located on the bank of the Rupsha River. It is considered the gateway to the Sundarbans.

Barishal

Barisal became municipality in 1957 and remains until 2002 when it was upgraded into a city corporation.
